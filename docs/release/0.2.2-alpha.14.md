## Known Notes

- This release proves the 5-line saved-fixture lifecycle and narrow-brush interaction path, not the future 5x100k stress variant.
- Total RSS remains a soft metric because WebView2, GPU, and runtime allocations are partly outside direct app control.
- A post-release audit found corrupted replacement glyphs in brush labels and one reagent fallback label. Those are UI-polish issues, not alpha.14 deploy blockers, and are tracked as a follow-up cleanup for the next candidate.
